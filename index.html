Sworannalayah Bridal Collection

Sworannalayah Bridal Collection is a premier jewellery destination located in the heart of Orleanpet, Puducherry, just opposite the Bus Stand near Chennai Silks on Maraimalai Adigal Salai. We specialise in stunning bridal jewellery sets, artificial ornaments, and occasion wear accessories that help brides and their families celebrate life's most cherished moments in style and elegance.

With a wide range of jewellery pieces spanning traditional South Indian bridal designs to contemporary fashion accessories, Sworannalayah is committed to offering beautiful adornments for every budget. Our store is open six days a week from 10 AM to 9 PM and on Sundays from 11 AM to 9 PM, ensuring you can visit us at your convenience. Whether you are preparing for a wedding, engagement, or festive celebration, we invite you to explore our curated collection and find the perfect piece for your special occasion.
